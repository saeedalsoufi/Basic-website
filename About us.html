MileEnd Funfair

MileEnd funfair is a company that's been formed in 2014 by the founder Saeed Alsoufi. Since 2014 we have been making amazing funfairs in MileEnd which is located in Mile end, Southroad, London E68xj72. Our funfair is open during summer time starting from May till August, in addition to eid. If you would like to contact us through the phone or to come visit us for a booking or to book online you can go to the contact us page and follow the instructions there.
The rides we offer:
Kids rides such as swings.
roller coasters.
Horror roller coasters with actors in it
Water rides.
Scary rides for the ones with a strong heart!
